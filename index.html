• < Programming passion >

    Programming became my passion, I prefer coding than watching movies or even reading, which I love.

    Someone once told me that reading code is like reading a novel, and there is indeed something in that. I also love writing (as an example you've seen my article on previous slide) so now I'm writing my own coding novels - I create characters that have impact on each other, their paths merge and then separate after the mission is accomplished. Sometimes they wait until the other is ready. Novels must have an introduction, a development and an ending - the introduction of the characters at the beginning, then the action and finally the obligatory happy ending without any unresolved conflicts ;)

    And, of course, the conduct of the story must not be convoluted so that the reader doesn't get lost ;)
